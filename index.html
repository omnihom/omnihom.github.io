Welcome!

Welcome to this website about Idiom Neutral, an international auxiliary language made public in 1902 by the International Academy of the Universal Language under the directorship of Waldemar Rosenberger, an engineer from Russia.

One can find here:

  • A grammar and dictionary of Idiom Neutral (1902)
  • Supplement to the dictionary (1906)
  • Historical letters written in Idiom Neutral (1904-1911)
  • Transcriptions of the journal Progres (1906-1908)
  • Information about Reform-Neutral, a later reform project of the language (1912)
